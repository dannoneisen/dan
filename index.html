1- Digital Marketing on Future Learn

It is a small, 2-weeks long course requiring 2 hours per week. The course covers some strategies and techniques of Digital Marketing for small business owners. If you are or want to be a Digital Marketer, you can use the same techniques to advance your career.

Topics

Heres is the breakdown of the topics that the course covers:

Week 1: Using Digital Marketing

  • Digital Marketing An Introduction
  • Display and Pay per Click Marketing

Week 2: Techniques of Digital Marketing

  • SEO
  • Email Marketing
  • Integrating Techniques

Why should you take the course?

If you are a beginner, this course is for you. Learn the importance of applying Digital Marketing Techniques to small companies.

Youd also learn the types of Digital Marketing, and their value. You would learn about the basics of designing a digital marketing plan for your business (or your bosss).

The right way of targeting the right clients with the available data is the most important learning outcome of this course. The cherry on top, youd learn the value and techniques of having an integrated Marketing Strategy.

When can you start?

The next batch for this course is due on 27th April 2020. However, you can enroll right away FOR FREE.

This is a useful guide for anyone who is not much familiar with digital marketing.

With the help of examples, case studies, and other useful content, learn to apply Digital Marketing to your business.

4- Online Business: Digital Marketing For Success on Future Learn

This is a short course comprising 4 weeks only. You are required to give it only 2 hours each week. The course provides you with an advanced-level knowledge of driving sales through online marketing.

Topics

Heres an outline of the topics covered in the course:

  • Designing an integrated marketing plan
  • Types of Marketing and business-specific tools for each type
  • Comparison of Digital Marketing tools for ROI
  • Value of SEO
  • Evaluating audience behavior with the listening tools
  • Metrics you need to measure to gauge success

Why should you take the course?

This wouldnt really be called a beginner-level course. You should take it if you:

  • Plan to out-compete your competitors with Digital Marketing.
  • Want to be able to design a stellar integrated marketing strategy with the most appropriate channels.
  • Increase your reach to the right audience by using a mix of traditional and digital marketing.
